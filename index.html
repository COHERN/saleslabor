KEY TERMS
SALES ID
Auto-calculated % variance between Actual and Projection. Negative means running below plan.
VAR (VARIANCE)
Difference between Actual and Projection. Green = ahead of plan. Red = behind plan.
LY (LAST YEAR)
Same period from the prior year used for year-over-year comparison.
YOY (YEAR OVER YEAR)
How current performance compares to the same period last year.
ACT STP %
Actual Sales to Percent — each metric expressed as a % of total sales.
WTD / QTD
Week-to-Date / Quarter-to-Date — the period being measured.
SHRINK (LINE 05C)
Inventory loss from theft, waste, damage, or errors — expressed as % of sales.
LABOR (LINE 19)
Total payroll cost including wages and benefits — expressed as % of sales.
COGS (LINE 04)
Cost of Goods Sold — what was paid for the merchandise sold.
MARKDOWNS (LINE 05a/b)
Price reductions taken on merchandise to move inventory.
EBITDA (LINE 40)
Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization — the bottom line gain.
PROPER PRODUCTION
% of fresh department production completed. Under 90% needs attention.
PRE-SUB OOS %
% of items substituted or out of stock before an order is filled. Over 3.7% means too many substitutions impacting customer experience.
ON TIME HAND OFF
% of orders handed off to the customer on time. Under 100% is bad.
ON TIME TILLING
% of orders completed on time. 90% means 10% of customer orders were late.
