READING VARIANCE
All variances shown as +/- only:
+$1,234 — you gained that much
-$1,234 — you lost that much
+0.25 pts — percentage points above target
-0.25 pts — percentage points below target
KEY TERMS
SHRINK = Inventory loss (theft, waste, damage, errors) as % of sales

LABOR = Total payroll cost (wages + benefits) as % of sales

WTD = Week-to-Date (current week only)
QTD = Quarter-to-Date (Jan 1 through today, cumulative)
